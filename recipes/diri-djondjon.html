Diri-Djondjon(Haitian black rice)

Ingredients

  • Jasmine rice
  • Maggie cubes(bouillon cubes)
  • Medium yellow onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Green Peas
  • Red bell pepper strings
  • Peanut oil or Olive oil

Directions

  1. Prepare all of the ingredients. Slice the red bell peppers, dice or chop the onions, mince the garlic, and open the Maggi.
  2. Sautee the onion, garlic for about 1 minute or until fragrant, then add the cube. Although 3 cubes are showing, I used for 4 cubes in the recipe. Dissolve the cubes completely by constantly stirring it.
  3. Meanwhile clean the rice in accordance to the instructions on the package. Once the cubes are dissolved, add the rice. Stir.
  4. Add the water and thyme. Stir just a couple times.
  5. The rice should look like this after you stir it. Now, cover the pot with the lid, turn the knob to sealing. Press the “Rice” button which will cook the rice for 12 minutes.
  6. After 12 minutes, quick release, and add the frozen peas and red bell peppers. Stir it all together.
  7. The peas will not bring down the temperature. I like to add the peas at the end as it helps the color to remain vibrant.
  8. The rice will look like this once everything is stirred together.
